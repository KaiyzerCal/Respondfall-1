

# Respondfall

## Revenue Recovery Network for Local Service Businesses

Respondfall captures the 35-40% of inbound calls that local service businesses miss every day — and converts that lost revenue into automated recovery sequences.

### What It Is

Not a missed call tool. That's what it does on day one. At scale, Respondfall is a multi-tenant SaaS platform that:

- Provisions local phone numbers automatically via Twilio
- Captures missed calls and fires recovery SMS sequences
- Qualifies leads with AI
- Generates reviews automatically
- Activates referral networks
- Shows revenue protected in real-time

### The Business Model

**Direct Tiers**
- Starter: $97/mo — Missed call SMS + recovery sequences
- Pro: $197/mo — + AI qualification + review automation  
- Agency: $397/mo — + referral automation + full analytics

**White Label**
- Call centers, franchises, multi-location operators run Respondfall under their own brand
- Custom pricing per partner
